    Defining the
    True Luxury Experience

    Set high in the Himalayas of Ladakh the Gawaling provides a luxury experience unlike any other. More than 3500 mtrs (11600 ft) above the sea level with fully local Ladakhi architecture and all the modern amenities is a picturesque Luxury hotel of Ladakh. The Gawaling is the perfect hideaway in the mountain providing you with a peaceful, calm and pleasant endeavor to make our guest fully immerse in the natural ecosystem and local culture.

    The Gawaling is an experiential hotel that offers 68 rooms including 03 Signature Rabsal Suites, 8 Superior Luxury Suites, 13 Super Deluxe Rooms, 16 Deluxe Rooms and 28 Standards Rooms.
